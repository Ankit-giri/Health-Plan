⚠️ Things to Avoid
Ye karo toh supplements bhi kaam nahi karenge
🚫
Tea / Coffee on empty stomach
Direct acid = jalan + acidity worsens immediately
🚫
Maida items (pizza, burger, naan)
Ferments in gut → gas, bloating, fat gain — daily worst offender
🚫
Cold drinks / soda
CO2 + acid = double gas + bloating. Switch to coconut water.
🚫
Deep fried outside food daily
Slows digestion, acid reflux, fat gain — max 2x week
🚫
Skipping breakfast
Empty stomach acid → jalan gets progressively worse
⚠️
Late night heavy meals (after 9pm)
Digestion slows at night → fat gain + gas + poor sleep
⚠️
Packaged chips / biscuits
Trans fat, excess salt = inflammation + water retention
⚠️
Sleep after midnight regularly
Cortisol spikes → fat gain, irritability, weakness stays

🧪 Rule Out — H. Pylori
Agar jalan daily ho raha hai toh H. Pylori infection possible hai. Bahar ka frequent khana = common cause. Simple stool test — ₹500-800 at Thyrocare. Positive hai toh doctor se 14-day antibiotic course. Supplements se nahi jayega.
